lichi

Litchi Vitamin C ka accha source hai, jo immune system ko majboot banane, skin health ko sudharne, aur free radicals se ladne mein madad karta hai. Isme Vitamin B6, niacin, riboflavin, aur folate bhi hoti hai, jo overall health ke liye zaroori hote hain.

Apple

Apple mein fiber, vitamins (jaise Vitamin C), aur antioxidants hoti hain jo overall health ke liye achhe hain. Ye digestion mein madad karti hain aur immune system ko boost karti hain.

Banana

Banana mein dietary fiber hota hai, jo digestion ko improve karta hai aur constipation ko rokne mein madad karta hai. Yeh fiber gut health ko support karta hai aur regular bowel movements ko promote karta hai.

Grapes

Grapes mein antioxidants, jaise resveratrol, flavonoids, aur tannins, hote hain jo free radicals se ladte hain aur oxidative stress ko kam karte hain. Yeh antioxidants aging aur chronic diseases ke risk ko kam karte hain..
